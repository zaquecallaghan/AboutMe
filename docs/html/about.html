Magical Moments Disney Guru

I have been a Disney enthusiast for my entire life. My family has been vacationing there as long as I can remember, and then we visited even more when we moved to Florida. I love the depth and imagination of the parks, leading me to create Park Pursuit, a digital scavenger hunt 6featuring those hard-to-find details that the careful observer would most appreciate.

I can often be found at the parks, especially for Dapper Days! Those are the times I get to put two of my favorite things together: being at Disney and wearing a clean suit!

Marching Band, Wind Ensemble, Jazz Band Musician

I started playing the saxophone when I was 8 years old. Since then I have had the opportunity of performing in my high school concert bands, jazz bands, and marching band. Those groups were invited to play at the state level competitions, where our concert bands and jazz bands each ranked as some of the top bands in the state of Florida. I was then accepted to be a member of the Cougar Marching Band at BYU, as well as a member of the ROC Band to cheer on BYU's Basketball team.

Chief Unpaid Choreographic Luminesence Engineering Intern Christmas Specialist

Back in 2015, my neighbors and I were selected to participate in ABC's The Great Christmas Light Fight. Over the years we've continued to expand our show and find new ways to excite our guests from around the world! We perform shows nightly for both Christmas and Halloween. Come check us out!
